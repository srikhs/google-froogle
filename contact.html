Jeppiaar Engineering College, IEEE Computer Society Chapter
in association with
IEEE Madras Section Computer Society
Proudly Presents
Google Froogle - A Google Product Insight Event

The IEEE Computer society crew takes immense pleasure in
announcing the "Google Froogle - A Google Product Insight Event" with
the Google Student ambassador Joses Paull of our College, for budding
Technical Aspirants to Showcase their Skills in Google Applications
and Products. Every Individual aspirant who has keen interest in
Google
Apps and Products, can participate in this event and prove your worth to the
Technical world.
We need your hand to portray the Epitome of blossoming Technological
Development in a Firm like Google and it's Products. This will further
enhance your Learning process regarding Google Application
Development.
We appreciate your active participation in this event to make it a huge success. Please Convey it to all 
your fellow mates so that the reach of this event achieves the maximum support from the Student
Community.

Date : 31st August - 1st September 2013

**Open to both IEEE and non IEEE Members.
**No prior registration required.
